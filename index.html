Online Social Experiment 온라인 사회 실험

This online social experiment is Six Degrees of Separation Experiment in online.
Participant enter and submit these.
1. Participant's First and Last Name
2. Participant's gender
3. Participant's age
4. Participant's nationality
5. Name of the person who gave the link to you
The goal of this experiment is to observe how far this experiment that started from one person extends.
No hacking and virus on this page
